Spoon's Adoption Story

My family and I adopted Spoons from a local animal shelter back in 2020 when he was just a 3-month-old kitten. He was energetic and loved stealing and eating all kinds of human food - even broccoli! In this photo, he was exploring the inside of my family's Little Free Library.

Spoon's Life Now

Spoons is now 3-years-old and is as silly and problematic as ever. His favorite activities include playing fetch, napping, and hanging out with his puppy friends.
